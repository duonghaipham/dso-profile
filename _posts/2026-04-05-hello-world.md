Every developer's first output is "Hello, World!" — so it felt right to start this blog the same way.

This site is built with Jekyll and hosted on GitHub Pages. The goal is to have a place to document what I'm learning, share projects, and think out loud in writing.

## Why Write?

Writing forces clarity. When I can't explain something in writing, it usually means I don't understand it as well as I thought. This blog is as much for me as it is for any reader.

## What to Expect

- Technical posts about tools, languages, and architecture decisions
- Retrospectives on projects I've built
- Notes on things I'm learning

Let's get started.
